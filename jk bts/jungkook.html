GOLDEN JUNGKOOK

INTRODUCTION 

Jungkook of BTS is a South Korean singer who is the group's youngest member and main vocalist.
Jungkook was born on September 1, 1997 in Busan, South Korea, and his real name is Jeon Jungkook.
Jungkook made his debut as a member of BTS in 2013, drawing attention for his outstanding vocal and dance skills on various music and stages of the group.
His sensational dancing and powerful voice are gaining huge popularity at home and abroad.

The reason I like BTS's Jungkook is because of his outstanding dancing skills, outstanding vocal skills, attractive appearance, and charisma. Additionally, his diverse abilities and human charm deeply impress fans. “Jungkook is versatile not only in music but also in art, and his hard work is a positive inspiration to his fans.”

Track List

Track 1 - 3D (feat. Jack Harlow)

Jungkook's attractive vocals are in perfect harmony with the catchy sound, which is repeated as an R&B pop song with minimal musical instrument composition and striking sophisticated rhythm in harmony with the old-school sound. It expresses the desire to reach the other person with the witty material of the eyes of 1, 2, and 3. American rapper Jack Harlow participated in the feature, and David Stewart, who participated in producer BloodPop and the BTS Dynamite, worked on it.

Track 2 - Closer to You (feat. Major Lazer)

A low-pitched synthesizer and soft Jungkook's vocals on the bass were added. It is an R&B genre with a reggaeton rhythm, which contains the hearts of two lovers who are irresistibly attracted to the song. Major Lazer, who became popular in the EDM music world, participated in the song.

Track 3 - Seven (feat. Latto) - Explicit Ver.

Amid the addictive melody, warm-sounding acoustic guitar and rhythm of the "UK garage" genre, Jungkook has enhanced the charm and completeness of the song with his sweet vocals. "Seven" is a passionate serenade with lyrics that he wants to be with his loved one all week.

Track 4 - Standing Next to You-TITLE

It is a modern reinterpretation of the old school sound of the disco funk genre, and it emits explosive energy by combining rich wind instrument performance, explosive percussion, and Jungkook's powerful singing voice. It is a song that pledges that "Our love is deeper than anything else, so we will be with you no matter what adversity comes." Andrew Watt, producer of "Seven (feat. Latto)," and Cirkut, regrouped to produce "Standing Next to You."

Track 5 - Yes or No

Ed Sheeran's unique catchy melody and guitar performance are impressive. A person who feels his or her current emotions are love conveys his or her overwhelming emotions by asking if he or she feels the same way as me. Blake Slatkin, who has worked with Circuit Cirkut, Sam Smith and The Kid LAROI along with Ed Sheeran, participated as a producer.

Track 6 - Standing Next to You-TITLE

Afropop, a genre that is loved around the world, has been completed as an electronic dance pop song that is easy to listen to by meeting DJ Snake. The synth with a sense of space harmonizes with Jungkook's vocals, raising the sensibility of the song. It contains the wish that "no matter how far we are between us, my heart that loves you will not change, so please don't change either."

Track 7 - Hate You

It is a pop ballad song that expresses deep emotions only with the Lofi-textured piano performance and Jungkook's voice. It sings the mournful heart of "I am trying to hate you because this love is so hard right now." Canadian singer-songwriter Shawn Mendes participated in the songwriting.

Track 8 - Somebody

An R&B song with an ear-catching dull bass and a mournful melody. He feels that his relationship with his lover has changed and says that the time has come to admit that he is not the one to be with.

Track 9 - Too Sad to Dance

It is a song that contains a confession that she dances and pretends to be okay after breaking up with her lover, but rather waits for her contact, far from being forgotten. With an acoustic pop song consisting of a guitar and keyboard, Jungkook expressed his sad feelings in a calm voice. Producer David Stewart participated in the song writing.

Track 10 - Shot Glass of Tears

It is a pop ballad song, starting with a monotonous piano, and adding a chorus to the performance of instruments such as guitar and bass to express the feelings of driving. It is a song about the fear that the heart that has been healed through the pain of separation will be able to feel a new feeling of love again someday.

Track 11 - Seven (feat. Latto) - Clean Ver.

Amid the addictive melody, warm-sounding acoustic guitar and rhythm of the "UK garage" genre, Jungkook has enhanced the charm and completeness of the song with his sweet vocals. "Seven" is a passionate serenade with lyrics that he wants to be with his loved one all week.
